Frequently Asked Questions
Is Rodish Exchange India legal? ⌄
We are registered with Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU) of India and 100% compliant with Indian regulations.
Do I need crypto to trade on Rodish Exchange India? ⌄
No! You can deposit INR directly from your bank account and start trading crypto futures and options without owning any cryptocurrency.
Can I buy Crypto on Rodish Exchange India? ⌄
Rodish Exchange India is a derivatives exchange. We offer Futures and Options on crypto assets. You can trade spot crypto on our platform as well.
What are the trading fees? ⌄
We offer zero fees on OTM options expiry, zero exit fee on futures via Scalper Offer, and zero entry fee on spot trading. Competitive rates for active traders.
